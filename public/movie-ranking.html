The Best Movies according to Jay

My top 3 movies of all-time.

End of Evangelion

This the greatest anime film. I loved the action sequences and the intricate symbolisms and imagery.

The Dark Knight

Powerhouse performances, especially by the late Heath Ledger; containing realistic action sequences and an amazing score.

Social Network

Directed by one of my favorite directors David Fincher; it contains one of the best written screenplays for a movie.
